AI in the Boardroom:
From Insight to
Action


June 24th 2026

AI in the Boardroom brings together leaders, directors, and governance experts to examine how AI, strategic foresight, and modern decision-making are reshaping board effectiveness. The event focuses on how boards navigate complexity, interpret emerging risks, and make high-stakes strategic decisions in an AI-driven environment.

The summit opens with a panel featuring Rt Hon. Justine Greening, former UK Cabinet Minister, Paul Johnston co-founder, AI Working Group for Company Secretaries and Dr. Sina Wulfmeyer, AI leader, moderated by Peter Swabey of the Chartered Governance Institute. The discussion will explore how AI is influencing board decision-making, regulatory expectations, and enterprise risk.

Sharon Constançon will bring to the panel a practical governance perspective, focusing on how AI risk and policy are translated into decisions that stand up to board-level scrutiny.

This is followed by Terri Le Couteur, who will share practical approaches to strengthening board effectiveness and building decision-making confidence in an AI-enabled environment.

In addition to the panel discussions, the summit includes a series of focused, practical workshops designed specifically for board directors and governance professionals. These sessions move beyond theory to address how AI and strategic decision-making are applied in practice.

Attendees will gain concrete tools, approaches, and governance perspectives they can take directly back into their organisations to strengthen board effectiveness and decision-making.
